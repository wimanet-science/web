## What are STSMs ? 
Short-term scientific missions (STSM) are exchange visits between researchers involved in a COST Action, allowing scientists to visit an institution or laboratory in another COST Member state  participating  in  WIMANET.  These  scientific  missions  have  a  maximum  budget  of  **EUR 4,000**,  and  a  **minimum  duration  of  7  days**.  Their  aim  is  to  foster  collaboration  in  excellent research infrastructures and share new techniques that may not be available in a participantâ€™s home institution or laboratory. 
  
## Who can apply? 
Short-term scientific missions are open to all members of WIMANET based in a COST Member state or NNC participating in WIMANET. 
 
## How to Apply? 
This round of applications for STSMs will close on **31st January 2026**; late applications may be considered if budget allows. 
Applications are first submitted on the [e-COST website](https://e-services.cost.eu/stsm), and must also be e-mailed to the Grant Awarding Co-ordinator, [Dr Alexandra  Conduneanu](alexandra.corduneanu@usamvcluj.ro) and the [WIMANET  e-mail](wimanet.science@gmail.com).

More information can be found in the [STSM Grant announcement document](https://github.com/wimanet-science/web/blob/ad3c13b7cd549a1c8c7acc856050e012b658c2b1/assets/docs/STSM%20Grants%20announcement.docx) online and the [Annotated Rules for COST Actions](https://www.cost.eu/uploads/2023/11/COST-094-21-Annotated-Rules-for-COST-Actions-Level-C-V1.4-Final-.pdf).
